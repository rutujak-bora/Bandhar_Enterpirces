Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common B2B local procurement, logistics, and GST-compliant tax invoicing questions in India.

Our Minimum Order Quantity (MOQ) depends on the product category. For commercial printers, the MOQ starts at 5 units. For laptops and high-performance workstations, it starts at 10 units. For smartphones and accessories, it starts at 50 units. We offer special wholesale pricing tiers for larger bulk shipments.

We manage all local state tax compliance, state-to-state e-way bills, and transit declarations. All billing invoices are 100% GST-compliant, allowing your business to claim seamless Input Tax Credit (ITC) for your IT infrastructure expenditure.

We offer two domestic options: Air Express and Surface Logistics. Air Express is recommended for urgent hardware needs, delivering within 1 to 3 business days to major metros. Surface Logistics (via road/rail) is optimized for large-scale corporate print setups, taking 5 to 7 business days.

We partner exclusively with brand-authorized national distributors and manufacturers in India. All hardware comes with official local manufacturer warranty coverages, eligible for direct support and diagnostics at authorized service centers across India.
